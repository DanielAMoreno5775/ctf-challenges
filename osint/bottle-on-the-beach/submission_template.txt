Challenge Name: Bottle on the Beach

Intended Category: OSINT

Credit To: Daniel Moreno

Description: 
Recently, I was walking Pensacola's beaches and found this beer bottle. Could you help me find where and when it was made? 

Submit the flag with the following format: PCA{NumberOfNearestHighway_City_Country_YYYY_MM_DD}.

Hints:
* Sol Cerveza is owned and made by Molson Coors. Do they offer any help in reading the batch code?
* Cuauhtémoc Moctezuma Brewery operates the plants that manufacture Sol Cerveza.

Solution:
1. Look at the image to determine the brand
2. Look up Sol's batch codes for primary packaging, which leads to [this](https://www.molsoncoors.com/sites/molsonco/files/How%20to%20Read%20Code%20Dates_vFinal%20%281%29_0.pdf)
3. Determine that the numbers in the image are L6094484EA1204 and BBD AUG 2026
4. Since the document says that best-before dates are always within 9-26 weeks of production, we can assume that the year refers to 2026
5. Using that information, set the date as 26094
6. Convert that date from Julian date format to obtain the production date
7. Use the document to map the brewery code to a specific city
8. Next, figure out what company operates the brewery. This can easily be done via [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cuauht%C3%A9moc_Moctezuma_Brewery), [Tavern Trove](https://www.taverntrove.com/moctezuma-brewery-of-orizaba-veracruz-mexico-br-5762.html), or plenty of other websites
9. With that name, go to Google and open up the site in Google Maps
10. Scroll out and see that the factory is one intersection away from Mexican Federal Highway 150

Flag: `PCA{150_Orizaba_Mexico_2026_04_04}`
