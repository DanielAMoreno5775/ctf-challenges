Challenge Name: Blue Angel Cipher

Intended Category: Crypto

Credit To: Daniel Moreno

Description: 
The Blue Angels are known for their skillful flying. However, I'm sure that someone's hidden a covert message amongst their flights.

Hints:
* QR codes are just binary data. Binary can be anything.

Solution:
1. Skim through the parts of the video where they are flying.
2. Find the QR code (which is up around the 0:50 mark) and take a screenshot of it.
3. Paste that screenshot in Google Lens to extract the text.
4. Convert the phonetic alphabet into the flag.

Flag: `PCA{1_h34r_qr_c0d3s_ar3_ju57_b1n42y}`
